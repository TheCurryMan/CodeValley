Coding Bootcamps for Middle Schoolers



Middle School is the perfect time to expose students to the world of coding. Get your kids a jumpstart in the tech industry by enrolling them into Code Valley. Spend a little time over summer to gain life-long benefits. Learn the essentials of programming from highly qualified and passionate high schoolers.

Learn

Students will learn the essential tools of App Development such as user-interface elements, API's, and other computational logic and algorithms.

Build

By the end of the course, Students will be able to develop mobile applications and games. Throughout the course, students will build real sample apps to test and develop their skills.

Launch

Code Valley will put your students on the path to launching future mobile application and game projects into the market.
